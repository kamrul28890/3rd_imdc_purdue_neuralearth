Dear team leaders,

We are happy to share some good news!

A new dataset has been added to the IMDC 2026 data repository and is now available to all participants through the IMDC website.

The Afya Application Data dataset has recently been incorporated into the collection of resources available for model development and evaluation. We would like to express our sincere gratitude to the Afya team for making these data available to the community and for supporting the challenge.

As a reminder, the Sprint data repository currently includes a variety of datasets that may be useful for your forecasting models, including epidemiological, climate, demographic, and other complementary data sources. We encourage all teams to explore the available resources and assess whether they may add value to your modeling approaches.

You can access the full list of datasets here:

https://sprint.mosqlimate.org/data/

We hope this new resource proves useful for your work, and we are looking forward to seeing the innovative approaches that emerge throughout the challenge.

As always, if you have any questions, feel free to contact us.

Best wishes,

The IMDC Organizing Committee
